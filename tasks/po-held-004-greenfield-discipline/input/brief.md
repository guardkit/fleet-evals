# Brief: RoundRoute

We run delivery rounds for a group of community pharmacies. Drivers collect dispensed
prescriptions in the morning and deliver them to housebound patients during the day.
Today the rounds are planned on a whiteboard and paper manifests; proof of delivery is
a signature on a clipboard.

We want software that plans each driver's daily round, gives the driver a manifest they
can work through on the road, records proof of delivery, and lets the pharmacy see
where a prescription is. Some medicines are temperature-sensitive and some are
controlled drugs, and missed deliveries have to be returned to the pharmacy the same
day.

That's the whole brief — assume what you must, and say so.
